Our Characters

Bard

Bard Bubbard is a newspaper editor from BuzzNews. He works in the company for 5 years with no holiday after his late wife, Diana, died of brain tumor. Her death changed his life for good.

Betty

Betty Anderson is BuzzNews front office staff. She is a good friend that takes care of Bard Bubbard after she knows that he lost his wife. She have a little crush to Bard, but not knowing how to help him but to comfort him.

Bob

Bob Anderson is BuzzNews Editing Team Manager. He is the boss of Bard, and father of Bard's friend, Betty, who works at the front office. As a good boss and a good friend of Bard, he is the one to notice that Bard have to get some rest or vacation to ease his mind.

Synopsis

Map of The Soul is a story of Bard Bubbard, a newspaper editor in BuzzNews, who worked there for years and rarely took his day off from his job after the loss of his late wife. This action started to cause him to lose his sense of reality, losing the colors of the world he sees.
His adventure would include combat with the monster of his darkness. Venturing across the land of the soul with a map in one hand and a magical pen on the other to seek and return the color of his world to its place.
His adventure would end with him waking up from his sleep, realizing that it was all a dream. But the color he sees seeping back to his perspective, are real.
